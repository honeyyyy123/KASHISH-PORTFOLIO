Key Learnings

From my Personality Development (PESE 300) Course

UNIT 1: SWOC Analysis

This unit was a practical audit of my own skills. I learned to pinpoint my strong suits (like adaptability) and identify areas I need to improve (like time management). The real takeaway was learning how to use these strengths to grab new opportunities and create a plan to tackle my challenges.

UNIT 2: Self Introduction

This unit taught me to summarize my skills, goals, and passion into a clear and confident short length introduction, ensuring I make a strong and memorable first impression in any professional setting.

UNIT 3: Presentation Skills

This unit broke down the art of storytelling. I learned that a good presentation isn't just about the slides; it's about structuring a narrative. I practiced how to build a clear (Introduction, Body, Conclusion) flow and use visual aids (like graphs and images) to support my message, not replace it.

UNIT 4: Public Speaking

Through practical exercises like JAM (Just-a-Minute) sessions and extempore, I directly confronted stage fright. This unit gave me techniques to manage anxiety, organize my thoughts quickly under pressure, and speak clearly without relying on filler words (like 'um' or 'ah').

UNIT 5: Art of Conversation
